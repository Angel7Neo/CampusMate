Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who can use CampusMate services?

CampusMate is exclusively for registered CPUT students. You’ll need your student number to sign up, log in, and book appointments.

How do I book an appointment?

To book:
1️⃣ Sign up with your student number and email.
2️⃣ Log in using your credentials.
3️⃣ Go to the Booking Page and choose your service, date, and time.
4️⃣ Confirm your booking and view it in your student profile.

Do I need to sign up before booking?

Yes. Every student must sign up first before they can log in and book appointments. This ensures only CPUT students access the system.

What services can I book?

Students can book services such as:
- General medical consultations
- Minor ailments (colds, flu, etc.)
- Reproductive health support
- HIV counselling and testing
- Treatment for STIs and more.
See the full list on the Booking Page.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Yes. Log into your student profile to check your appointment status and reschedule or cancel if needed.

What are the clinic operating hours?

⏰ Monday - Friday: 8:30 am - 5pm
⏰ Saturdays: 8am - 2pm
❌ Closed on public holidays.

How will I know if my booking is confirmed?

Once you confirm your booking, a success message will appear with your appointment details. You can also view it anytime in your student profile.
