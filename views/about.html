Who We Are

Campus Mate is designed for CPUT students to easily book clinic and counselling appointments on campus.

Our platform ensures students can quickly access healthcare services, reduce waiting times, and get timely support without disrupting their studies.

With Campus Mate, students can manage their wellbeing efficiently while staying connected to important campus resources.

Our Mission

To provide CPUT students with a seamless system for booking clinic and counselling appointments.

Our Vision

To be the trusted digital companion for campus healthcare and student wellbeing at CPUT.

Our Values

Accessibility, Efficiency, Care, and Student-Centered Support.
