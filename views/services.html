Your Health, Our Priority

At CampusMate Health Services, we provide students with professional, accessible, and confidential healthcare. From consultations to counseling, we’ve got you covered.

Services We Offer

  • General medical consultations + basic medication
  • Minor ailments (colds, sore throats, diarrhea)
  • Reproductive health (morning after pill included)
  • HIV counselling & testing (VCT)
  • Nutritional & counselling support for HIV positive students
  • Treatment of sexually transmitted infections
  • TB-DOTS supervision
  • Wound dressings & minor injuries
  • Health education programmes
